
**NFO**: New Fund Offer
**SEBI**: Securities and Exchange Board of India
**AMFI** : Association of Mutual Funds in India
**Mkt cap**: Share price * No of shares

**SBI MULTICAP FUND:**
25% Large cap + 25% Mid cap + 25% Small cap + remaining 25% is decided by Fund manager

### Large cap: 
* Stable funds(less downside risk)
* Decided based on Mkt cap
* Top 100 companies
### Mid cap: 
* Growth potential
* 101 - 250 in AMFI
### Small cap: 
* Risk 
* Any company below 250 comes under this
### Who decides Large/Mid/Small cap
* Decided by AMFI website
